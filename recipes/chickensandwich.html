Chicken Sandwhich

Description

This recipe is for a spicy chicken sandwich, not for the feint hearted!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Chicken
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbs Siracha
  • Lemon
  • Briouche bun
  • 2 tbs Mayonaise
  • 1/2 Tomato

Steps

  1. Cook chicken for 35 minutes
  2. Mix salt, lemon mayonaise and siracha in bowl
  3. Add bowl of sauce to chicken
  4. Cook chicken for further 10 minutes
  5. Toast briouche buns
  6. Cut tomato and place on toasted bun with extra mayo and siracha
  7. Serve and enjoy!
